Who we are!

The Otaku Smoke n' Brew Cook Team is a group of folks in the Lewisville, TX area that are obsessive about brewing beer and smoking BBQ..

Head Cook Jim Lavin started the team in 2015 after spending a couple years as a KCBS Certified Judge, and attending the Jack's Old South Cooking School to learn about competitions.

Now you can usually find the team at any of the local DFW Area KCBS competitions, relaxing by their smokers putting out some great BBQ while enjoying a good cigar and sipping scotch.

If the team's not competing they are probably smoking a whole hog for friends or family.
